Toy Helicopter CAD Modeling & Drawing Package

SolidWorks modeling project focused on accurate geometry and engineering drawings.
Reverse-engineered CAD model of a toy helicopter, created in SolidWorks using a physical reference object, measured dimensions, and multi-view photo documentation.
As part of a Computer Graphics and Design midterm project, I developed a fully detailed 3D model by first planning the build strategy, collecting key measurements, and then constructing complex geometry using surface lofts, sweeps, and precision extrusions.
After completing the CAD, I produced a representative engineering drawing packet with orthographic views and annotated dimensions to communicate the design in a manufacturing-ready format. This project strengthened my ability to translate real-world objects into clean parametric CAD and professional drawing sets.
